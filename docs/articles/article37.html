Giorgia Meloni, the President of the Council, appeared before the Court of Honor of the Chamber for a hearing that lasted just over an hour. The controversy was sparked by Giuseppe Conte, leader of the M5s, who had accused the premier of making false accusations against him in connection with the "Mes" affair. During the hearing, Meloni answered all of the questions asked by the members of the court. She denied any wrongdoing and stated that her actions were taken in the best interests of the Italian people. The court then adjourned to consider the evidence presented during the hearing before making a final decision.
